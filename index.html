Give n Receive

For thousands of years, what you produced defined your worth.

Muscle. Mind. Creativity.

AI is absorbing all of it.

What remains is what AI cannot do —

the purpose and meaning we create for each other.

The neighbor who hosts the block party.

The friend who turns neighbors into community.

The player who gives the game its heart.

The one who walks into a room and makes it better.

What makes us most human.

Never measured. Never reciprocated.

n

We measure what you give to others, just by being you.

Your presence. Your energy. Your generosity.

Our economy after AI is not about what you produce.

It is about who you are to the people around you.
